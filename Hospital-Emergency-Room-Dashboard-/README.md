**📊 Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard **

Developed an Excel-based ER Dashboard to monitor and optimize emergency department operations.

**Key Metrics:**
• 485 patients served
• 35.51 min average wait time
• 4.71 patient experience score
• 48% admission rate

**Dashboard Features:**
✅ Patient flow tracking and admission status
✅ Wait time monitoring and attendance patterns
✅ Age group distribution (highest: 20-29 years)
✅ Department-wise referral analysis
✅ Gender-based service insights

**Impact:**
• Better resource allocation during peak hours
• Improved patient experience monitoring
• Data-driven staff planning
• Enhanced coordination across departments
• Monthly performance comparison (2023 vs 2024)

🛠️ Technical Details

Tool: Microsoft Excel
Visualizations: Charts, graphs, and KPI cards
Data Analysis: Pivot tables and formulas
Interactivity: Year selector and monthly filters

This dashboard demonstrates how Excel can effectively transform healthcare data into actionable insights for better patient care.
